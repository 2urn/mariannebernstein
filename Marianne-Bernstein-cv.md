*Nomadicube* is a participatory and collaborative project by Marianne Bernstein. A 10’ cube becomes a roving satellite, living room, radio station, studio, performance space, and movie theatre; collecting stories and bringing people together through spontaneous and intimate interactions, often around social justice issues.  
Past cubes include: *The Welcome House* (2010), Love Park; *Not a Vacant Lot* (2011), Philadelphia; *Beyond What Was* (2012), New Haven; Skagaströnd, Iceland (2014).
